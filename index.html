About Omega Transport

Our Vision

To improve the quality of social and commercial transport to benefit everyone. We are committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and innovative solutions specifically tailored to the needs of our clients.

Our Story

Omega Transport has been in the loop since 2015. We sprung from a drive to make sure the children who attend Omega Life's school have a safe, predictable, comfortable and practical way to come to class.

We first served Foundations for Life — a school for children with special educational needs. Having achieved this and possessing the necessary vehicles, we branched out to offer services on a commercial basis, extending our attitude of care to the wider public.

The Omega Life Family

Omega Transport is run by Omega Life — a company that umbrellas other businesses focusing on bettering lives. We have education, training, talent, digital, construction and care services. We proudly own and maintain 15 care homes for children across the UK.

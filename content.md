## About Us

### Welcome to Omega Transportation

We hire out transportation for various personal and commercial purposes in the North West. Our range of pickup locations are generally within a 1 hour drive from Liverpool, but exceptions may be made and clarification on whether a certain pickup location is acceptable will be available over the phone. ### Our Vision

To improve the quality of social and commercial transport to benefit everyone. Omega Transport sprung from a drive to make sure the children who attend Omega Life's school have a safe, predictable, comfortable and practical way to come to class.

### Our Story

Omega Transport has been in the loop since 2015. Omega Transport is ran by Omega Life — a company that umbrellas other businesses focusing on bettering lives. We have education, training, talent, digital, construction and care services.

Omega Transportation first served our school Foundations for Life with priority, a school that understands the flexibility and support required for teaching neurodiverse children and children who had experienced adverse childhood experiences. We had a solid foundation to begin with so we were eager to grow even further.

Since seeing the impact our vehicle service has on our young people, getting them from A to B comfortably and efficiently, we opened our service up to the public.

In 2024, Omega Care Group opened a school for children with special educational needs — there being a shortage of SEN schools — thus good transport for kids coming from a wide range of areas, suitable for various needs, was essential.

Having achieved this and possessing the necessary vehicles, Omega decided to branch out and offer our services on a commercial basis, extending our attitude of care out to the wider consumer base.

## Storyboard Direction

**Camera:** Third-person GTA follow-cam. Bus always visible. User feels like they're driving.

### Shot Flow

1. **Hero intro** — Omega bus approaching cinematically (outside, current shot 1 style)
2. **Get behind the wheel** — Camera sweeps behind the bus into GTA follow-cam position
3. **Drive to the Omega lot** — Bus drives into the Omega Transport parking lot / garage
4. **Fleet showcase** — User drives through the lot, sees all vehicle options parked up (17-seater, 7-seater, people carrier, Mercedes)
5. **About Omega** — Bus drives past Omega building / signage / branding. The story unfolds
6. **Contact** — Bus arrives at the Omega office entrance. Address and details visible in the scene

**Key principle:** The bus is the guide. It takes the user to each section. Content is part of the world, not floating overlays.
